Our Overhaul Process

1. Diagnosis

Initial evaluation of compressor condition, performance history and failure symptoms.

2. Disassembly & Inspection

Complete teardown with detailed measurement and inspection of all critical components.

3. Reassembly & Parts Sourcing

Sourcing of OEM or aftermarket parts and precision reassembly to manufacturer tolerances.

4. Testing & Startup

Performance testing and on-site startup support to confirm reliable operation.

Compressor Types We Overhaul

Overhaul and repair services for the compressor types most widely used in industrial refrigeration.

Screw Compressors

Rotor, bearing and slide valve overhaul for Frick, Mycom, Howden, Bitzer and Vilter units.

Reciprocating Compressors

Valve plate, ring, piston and crankshaft repair and restoration.

Oil-Flooded Systems

Oil separator inspection and oil management system restoration.

Capacity Control Systems

Slide valve, VFD and unloader system inspection and repair.

Motors & Couplings

Alignment, coupling replacement and motor condition assessment.

Suction & Discharge Valves

Inspection, reconditioning and replacement of valve assemblies.
